Spellnook — Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Spellnook is a word‑puzzle game at spellnook.ankur.theworkpc.com. This policy explains what we collect and why. We keep it minimal.

What we collect

  • If you sign in with Google: your name, email address, and profile picture URL, as provided by Google Sign‑In. We do not receive your Google password.
  • Gameplay data: your game results (mode, word length, win/loss, number of guesses, timing, date) used to compute your stats, streaks, and the leaderboard.
  • Local storage in your browser: your theme preference, a guest session id, and your in‑progress / completed daily game, so the game works across refreshes. This stays on your device.

How we use it

  • To provide the game, your account, statistics, and the leaderboard (which shows your display name and avatar).
  • We do not sell your data or use it for advertising.

Sharing

We use Google Sign‑In for authentication (see Google's Privacy Policy). Your display name and avatar are visible to other players on the leaderboard. We don't share your email with anyone.

Data retention & deletion

Account and game data are stored on our server until you request deletion. To delete your account and data, contact us at the address below. You can also play as a guest without signing in.

Contact

Questions or deletion requests: privacy@spellnook.example (update with your real contact).
